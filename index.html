About us

We started RetroCBO after seeing the same problem over and over: stunning retreat venues with incredible potential sitting half-empty during the week, while corporate teams are desperately searching for offsite locations.

The disconnect? Venue owners don't have outbound sales systems. They rely on listing platforms, word of mouth, and wedding season. Meanwhile, HR managers, EAs, and ops leads are actively planning retreats — but nobody's reaching them.

We bridge that gap. Using enterprise-grade GTM systems proven at 9-figure B2B companies, we identify, reach, and book corporate clients for your venue — so you can focus on hosting unforgettable experiences.

If this is you, feel free to book a call. Always happy to help, even if we don't end up working together.
